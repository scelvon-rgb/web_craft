ABOUT

A moment of pride and national recognition unfolded at State House, Nairobi, as the Kabarak University President s Award Kenya (PA-K) unit joined other achievers for the prestigious Gold Award Presentation Ceremony. The team was led by our Vice-Chancellor and Patron of Kabarak….

history

Kabarak University was established by the second President of Kenya, H.E. Daniel Toroitich Arap Moi, This resulted from his visionary idea of setting up a Christian University that would meet the demand for higher education in Kenya and offer quality education based on strong moral principles.

location

The Main campus of Kabarak University is located 20 Kilometres (12mi) from Nakuru City CBD, along the Nakuru – Eldama Ravine road.
